# sREYA Additional Utility

Beyond the benefits described above, sREYA has a growing set of use cases for everyday participants on Reya. Staking REYA to mint sREYA can lower trading costs for those on Premium Accounts, through a tiered fee schedule, with tiers determined by the amount staked. In the medium-term, sREYA will also be recognized as collateral for trading on Reya, meaning positions backed by sREYA follow the same liquidation framework as other collateral types.
